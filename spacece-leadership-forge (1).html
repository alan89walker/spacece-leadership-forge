Our Mission

We Don't Need Task Completers.
We Need System Changers.

Most internships are designed to test patience. This program is designed to test judgment, resilience, and the ability to bring clarity to uncertain situations.

At SpacECE India Foundation, the focus is not just on completing assigned work, but on developing individuals who can think independently and act decisively.

What is this internship?

A leadership-focused internship where real-world problems are used to build decision-making ability and ownership. Your role may belong to HR, technology, management, or design — but the real objective is leadership development.

Duration

1–12

Months, based on performance and progression. Short-term, mid-term, and long-term tracks available across all roles.

Mode

Fully Virtual

Full-time engagement. Your laptop, your space — but the accountability is 100% real.

What you gain

Real project experience · Exposure to cross-functional work · Leadership mindset development · System thinking ability · Pre-placement offer opportunities.

FAQ

Common Questions

Is it a paid internship?

Yes. This is a paid internship after 3 months. Candidates work as unpaid interns for the first 3 months, then the remaining period is paid. For sales teams, it is an incentive-based paid internship from Day 1.

Do you offer PPO?

Yes. We offer Pre-Placement Offers to our interns after a due selection process. Packages are offered based on performance, skill sets, position, and market standards.

What are the working hours?

We believe in flexible hours — you are not expected to log in at a fixed time. But you will be expected to complete daily assigned tasks and attend official meetings.

Will I get a Letter of Recommendation?

A LoR is not for everyone. You receive it only in exceptional cases when you perform beyond expectations and achieve significant milestones during the internship.

How many hours per day?

You will be assigned daily tasks and completion of those tasks is what matters. You are assessed by output and quality, not by the number of hours clocked.

Is it fully remote?

Yes. All internships are organized in a virtual working mode. You will need a personal laptop, reliable internet access, and a dedicated working setup.
